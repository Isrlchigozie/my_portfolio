GitHub Repo

Project Overview

HustleNet was conceived to address the unique challenges faced by freelancers in emerging markets, particularly in Africa. While global freelance platforms exist, they often don't cater to the specific needs, payment methods, and trust dynamics of these regions.

This platform provides a tailored solution with localized payment options, culturally relevant design, and a strong emphasis on community building alongside professional services. The integrated escrow system ensures security for both clients and freelancers, building trust in every transaction.
